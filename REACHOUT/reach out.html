WHAT WE DO

Reach out provides care to children who find it difficult
to access good education and health

orphan care

Reach out cares for up to 1000 orphaned/vulnerable childrfen and youth by surrounding them with unconditional love and support. We aim to create an environment in which they can flourish by providing a home, a family and a profound sense of belonging

medical intervention

Most under priviledge children suffer health problems, we have onsite medical facilities and personel tha provide the necessary treatment to keep our children as healthy as possible

We are the children's family and therefore have a responsibility to provide them with the skills they need to grow and develop into functioning members of society.This includes providing the children with a quality education and practical kill training and support as they treansition into society
